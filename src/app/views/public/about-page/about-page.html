About Ariel

Transforming Customer Relationships
Through World-Class Post-Sale Strategies

Ariel Benzakein is the founder of RetentionForge, a consultancy dedicated to helping SaaS and technology organizations retain and expand customer relationships by developing world-class post-sale teams and strategies.

With 25 years of experience in the tech industry, Ariel has built a global reputation for architecting scalable Customer Success, Professional Services, Technical Support, and Account Management functions that drive both retention and revenue growth.

He is widely recognized for building Experian’s first B2B SaaS Customer Success organization and for creating proven frameworks that continue to serve as operational playbooks for companies seeking sustainable, customer-centric growth.
